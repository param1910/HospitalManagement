What is Cardiology?


Derived from the Greek words, “Cardia” meaning heart and “logy” that means “study of”, cardiology is the branch of medicine that deals with the defects and diseases associated with the heart. Such diseases are managed by cardiologists. Max Hospitals have team of best cardiologists in India. The surgery, however, is performed by heart surgeons who specialize in surgical procedures to correct heart problems. Types of Cardiovascular Diseases


Most of the heart problems occur due to structural problems, blood clots and damaged vessels. Some of the most common types of cardiovascular diseases include: Angina, Arrhythmia, Heart Attack, Heart Failure, Rheumatic Heart Disease, Congenital Heart Disease and Valvular Heart Disease


Diagnosis
